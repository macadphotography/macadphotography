I am a wedding photojournalist. My aim is to capture the candid, in-between
moments — the ones that happen when no one is looking — as unobtrusively as I
can. I believe the best photographs are the ones you did not know were being
taken.

For portraiture, I work with handheld lighting equipment to shape light around
a person wherever we are. I see the best in people all the time, and my job is
simply to show it back to them. Emotions and expressions matter far more to me
than any backdrop.

> A photo should look good, but more importantly, it should be timeless.

I am endlessly inspired by photographers such as Marriane Chua, Lena Mirisola
and Kevin Mullin.

or let's [have a chat](/contact/).
