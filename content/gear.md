Over the years I have transitioned from Nikon DSLRs — the D300, D700, D800 and
D850 — to the Fujifilm system: the X-T2, X-T3, X-H1 and X-T30. The move was
about portability and fewer lens changes, letting me stay light on my feet and
present in the moment.

Fujifilm is also known for its beautiful colour science and versatile film
simulations. My photographs have an organic feel that, to my eye, cannot quite
be replicated with other camera systems.

I have also pioneered a lighting setup specifically for pre-wedding and
engagement shoots. If you would like the detailed rundown of what is in my bag,
just [get in touch](/contact/).
