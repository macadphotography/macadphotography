## Engagement

2, 3 and 6-hour packages are available for pre-wedding, engagement and portrait
shoots, including sessions across multiple locations. Deliverables are thumbnail
proofs together with high-resolution JPEGs. Album design is available on
request, and custom packages can be arranged. [Get in touch](/contact/) for
current details.

## Actual Day

Hourly, 4, 6 and 10-hour packages are available for actual-day wedding coverage.
As availability and details change through the year, please [contact me](/contact/)
for current pricing and options.

## Portraiture

1, 2 or 3-hour sessions for studio and/or outdoor locations. When enquiring,
let me know how you intend to use the images — Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn,
corporate websites, and so on — so I can tailor the session to suit.
